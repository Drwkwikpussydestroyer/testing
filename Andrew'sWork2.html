WELCOME TO ANDREW'S PORTFOLIO

Hi, I am Andrew and i made this website. I wanted to showcase what i've learned and experienced in one work, and so i made this. I initially wasn't a huge fan of html and css, eventually i got the hang of it, and so i incorporated my learnings in this work. I'm sure there are many mistakes and inaccuracies you may see but that's point; to learn by falling short. I guess that's a good metaphor for myself as creator of this website and as a person. i really like being exposed to things i'm unfamiliar of, and html, css, and javascript is one them. I hope you'll like my work and provide valuable insights for me to improve.
